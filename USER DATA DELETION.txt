User Data Deletion Policy — Emoterong Panda

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

At Emoterong Panda, we respect your privacy and provide you with the right to request the deletion of any personal data that Facebook makes available to Page administrators.

Because this is a Facebook Page, we do not collect or store personal data outside of Facebook. However, you may request removal of any personal information you believe is visible on the Page or within our interactions.

1. What Data Can Be Deleted

You may request the deletion of:

Messages you previously sent to the Page

Comments you posted on Emoterong Panda content

Any personal information contained within your interactions

Any screenshots or user-submitted content that belongs to you

Note:
We cannot delete data that is stored and controlled directly by Facebook, but you may manage or delete that information through your own Facebook account settings.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

You may contact us using any of the methods below:

✅ Option 1: Facebook Messenger

Message Emoterong Panda directly and state:
“I would like to request deletion of my data.”

✅ Option 2: Comment on the Page

You may comment or request a direct message, and we will assist you privately.

✅ Option 3: Delete Your Own Data

You may manually remove:

Your comments

Your reactions

Your shares

Any content you posted on the Page

Facebook also allows you to delete your account entirely if desired.

3. How We Process Deletion Requests

Once we receive your request:

We will review your message or profile.

We will remove any personal information visible on our Page that we can control.

You will receive confirmation that your data has been deleted.

Requests are usually processed within 48–72 hours.

4. Data We Cannot Delete

We cannot delete:

Information stored by Facebook itself

Posts or comments on other pages not managed by us

Data that Facebook does not provide access to for Page admins

For Facebook-controlled data, please refer to:

Facebook Data Policy: https://www.facebook.com/policy.php

5. Contact

If you wish to delete your data, please contact us through:

Facebook Messenger: Emoterong Panda

Or message us directly on the Page
